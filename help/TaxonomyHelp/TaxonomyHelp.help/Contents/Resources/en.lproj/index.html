Research Taxonomy Library

AI-powered market research and taxonomy builder for discovering, researching, classifying, and organizing companies into categories.

Getting Started

  • Create a project — Click the "+" card on the project selection screen or use ⌘N.
  • Add companies — Go to the Process tab to add company URLs for AI research.
  • Organize — Use the Taxonomy tab to create categories and subcategories.
  • Research — The Research tab shows AI-generated analysis for each company.
  • Discover — The Discovery tab finds new companies in your market.

Tabs

  • Companies — Browse, search, filter, and manage companies. Star favorites, assign categories, add tags.
  • Taxonomy — Visual category hierarchy. Drag to reorder. View as tree, graph, or knowledge graph.
  • Map — Geographic visualization of companies on an interactive map.
  • Research — AI-generated research reports and analysis for each company.
  • Canvas — Freeform visual workspace for arranging companies and notes.
  • Discovery — AI-powered company discovery and gap analysis.
  • Process — Batch operations: add URLs, run AI research, enrich data.
  • Export — Export data as JSON, CSV, Excel, or Markdown. Manage duplicates.
  • Settings — Configure AI backend, API keys, and app preferences.

AI Features

  • Company Research — AI analyzes company websites to extract description, pricing, features, and more.
  • Auto-Classification — AI suggests category assignments based on company descriptions.
  • Discovery — AI finds similar companies and identifies market gaps.
  • Dimensions — AI proposes custom research dimensions and populates them across companies.

Data & Backups

  • Data is stored locally in SQLite.
  • Automatic daily backups on app launch.
  • Manual backups via File > Backup Database.
  • Git sync available via File > Sync to Git.
